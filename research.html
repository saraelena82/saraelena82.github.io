Internships

During the summer of 2008, I had the privilege of doing an internship at Microsoft Research in the campus located at Redmond, WA. I was part of the MSR LATAM program -- where my generation was the first one to include women -- and had the opportunity of working with the Natural Language Processing (NLP) group, where Lucy Vanderwende was my mentor. My research during the internship was focused on (automatic) Web page summarization.
